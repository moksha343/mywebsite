COMMERCE

Commerce stream is one of the well-structured courses for students to start their career plan.The main factor of the Commerce stream is the diversity that it offers. If students are excellent at dealing with numbers, data, have a basic knowledge of economics and finance, then choosing commerce as a subject will be the smart choice. Commerce as a subject is defined as a study of trade and business activities, dealing with accounting and financial activities. The major subjects taught in Commerce stream are Accountancy, Economics, and Business Studies. Students with a genuine interest in the following subjects must choose this stream.

Commerce Stream Subjects


The major subjects of the commerce stream are as follows:


  1. Accountancy


    Accountancy is one of the most important subjects of the Commerce stream. It is a branch of mathematical science that is concerned with the financial information of companies, business entities, etc. The principles of accountancy are applied to business entities in three divisions. They are accounting, bookkeeping, and auditing.


    BUSINESS STUDIES


    Business studies of commerce stream deal with the operation and organization of business enterprises. This subject explains how the structure and functioning of market operations, human resource management, etc. Studying business studies will help students to understand different practices used in firms. As a result, this can lead students to master running a business. Moreover, this subject prepare student for their professional life.

    Economics


    Economics is a subject which deals with the study of the production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services. These subjects are categorized into two parts. They are Microeconomics and Macroeconomics. Microeconomics deals with individual consumers such as households or firms, etc. Whereas macroeconomics deals with the economy as a whole. Studying economics helps students to learn how country works and its impact on business enterprises and industries. In the recent era, economic reasoning and its theories are increasing day by day. Therefore, studying this subject will help students to learn about social situations where there is no monetary consideration, such as politics, law, history, religion, and other social interactions.

    Top 4 Benefits of Choosing Commerce Stream

      4.Commerce will always in demand, whether it’s a recession or no recession period.

    Highest Paid Jobs for Commerce Stream Students


      Chartered Accountant


      A Chartered Accountant is one of the best career options for students with a commerce background. Students can easily get into this job by clearing three levels of exams, internship, and additional qualifications. This job offers to handle company accounts and finances. Moreover, Chartered Accountant is one of the highly-paid jobs in India. Also, the salary of CA is around 60to 7 lakh rupees per annum. The salary keeps on increasing as the qualification of an accountant increases.

      Investment Banker


      An investment banker carries the most important position in a business organization. Therefore, it is one of the best and highly salaried jobs in India. Since commerce stream students know accounts and finance, hence they can advise and suggest to the organization regarding investments and savings. The major work of the investment banker is to assist in financial recommendations in a business so that they efficiently use their resources.


      Financial Planner


      The job of a financial planner is to plan the company’s resources to meet the short-term and long-term goals. The financial planner cures the financial health of a company. This is one of the highest-paid jobs after 12th for students with a commerce stream.


      BANKING


      Banking is one of the most common and demanding careers in India. A student from a commerce stream will always prefer to get into this sector of the economy. Also, the demand for this job is increasing day by day because of the rapid growth in employment opportunities in the banking sector.

      Stock Broker


      This is a job which can be done only by the commerce background students who have a clear understanding of various financial terms. Students of commerce background can easily understand the complexities of various investing mechanisms. The various stockbroker jobs include financial advisor, dealer, investment advisor, fundamental analyst, equity advisor, and many more. Also, it is one of the highly paid jobs in India.
